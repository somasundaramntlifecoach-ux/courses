Real-Life Example: The Answer She Didn't Give

A junior team member came to Divya, visibly stressed, saying "I don't know how to handle this difficult client, can you just tell me what to say?" Divya's old habit would have been to draft the exact email herself. Instead, she asked: "What have you already tried?" and "What's the one thing you're most worried will happen?" Within ten minutes, the team member had worked out her own approach — one Divya wouldn't have thought of, but that fit her own communication style perfectly. The client responded well, and the team member handled two similar situations confidently on her own after that.
